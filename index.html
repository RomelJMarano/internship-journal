Growth & Learning

Reflections

Week 1 · Getting Started

My first week was nerve-wracking and exciting all at once. I learned quickly that no lesson plan survives first contact with students — flexibility is a teacher's most important tool.

Week 4 · Challenges

Managing a class of 20 students was a real challenge. Watching my cooperating teacher showed me that true discipline is built on genuine respect and care — not just rules.

Week 12 · Looking Back

By the final week, our classroom had become a real community. I felt my own growth clearly and knew — with absolute certainty — that teaching is my calling.
